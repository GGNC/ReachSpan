About the Game

        The Calamity scarred reality, leaving behind a fractured galaxy with many lost and forgotten. Far sectors shattered and anomalies appeared. The Great Confederation a conservative monolithic dominion with long traditions and the chaotic Free Union an unbridled coalition pursuing terrifying tech- now wage a relentless war across the stars. Beyond the core systems and regulated routes lies the Reach, a lawless expanse teeming with untold horrors and secrets. You are Spacemongers, a motley crew of foolhardy merchants and fighters who venture around, undertaking jobs, claiming bounties and seeking profits. But remember, morality is a luxury in this erratic cluster of misery!
